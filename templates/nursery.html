The next room must have been a nursery. Its windows are dusty, but enough moonlight falls in to give you a good look.

The wallpaper is pastel blue with white bunnies. The floor is completely covered with a carpet. It was probably white or cream in the day, but now it’s mostly grey, with a number of colorful stains all over it. It is slightly sticky.

By the window stands a large, wooden crib, with a number of white folded neatly inside. On top of the blankets sits a little with one of its ears frayed.

There is also a large dresser with a few pieces of children's clothing inside.

In the corner stands a big armchair in light blue color. Another once-white bunny sits in the chair. It seems absurdly big - it’s probably taller than you. It has only one eye made of a big black button.
