You enter the hall. It is dark, spacious and windy.

Your steps echo on the stone floor, as you take a few steps towards the center of the room.

The balustrade of the massive, decorative stairway cracks in the draft. Some of the balusters have broken, and fallen lay on the floor. To your right there is an old umbrella stand, with a in it.

The walls are decorated with torn wallpaper with flowery pattern. There are two sets of splendid carved doors on each wall, and a more narrow, plain door partially obscured by the great staircase.
