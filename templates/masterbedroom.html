This must have been a master bedroom. The room is very big, but still the large four-poster bed takes up almost a third of the space. The coverings are made from velvet and embroidered with golden thread, and the walls are covered in similar gold fabric.

The velvet had collected a lot of dust, and what must have been a bright and luxurious room in the days of its glory, is now very dark.

Close to the big, but equally dimmed and dusty window there is a small vanity table with a carved, filigree chair. A silvery is thrown across the chair.

On the windowsill you can see a few of different sizes, with dried out plants in them.
